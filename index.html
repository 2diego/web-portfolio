Citizen complaints, Olavarria 2021-2022

Analysis of raw data containing information from formal complaints filed by citizens in Olavarría during 2021 and 2022. The objective of the analysis is to identify trends, including the monthly frequency of complaints, the public services receiving the highest number of reports, situations with the highest report frequency for each service, and the overall frequency of reports for each situation. It's worth noting that the dataset categorizes complaints (e.g., "Animal on the street", "Animal in unhealthy conditions", "Animal attack") based on the 'situation' (e.g., "Domestic Animal", "Wild Animal") and the responsible area or 'service' (e.g., "Animal Control").

Registered business in Olavarría, from 2018 to 2022

The objective of this project is to identify the productive sectors (such as 'commerce,' 'construction,' 'mining,' 'industry') with the highest number of authorized businesses from 2018 to 2022 in the city of Olavarria, and to analyze the variation over this period. Additionally, the project aims to determine the most developed economic activities. This type of analysis can be valuable for investment decision-making or for designing public policies aimed at the economic development of the city. For instance, it can help encourage the establishment, growth, and development of businesses in sectors such as industry, construction, and mining, which have a lower presence in the city. Alternatively, it can promote the specialization and development of business services, transportation, and storage, which are the economic activities with the highest number of authorized businesses.

  • Client: Personal project
  • Date: April 2023
  • Service: Data analysis and visualization
